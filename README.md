# 📊 Marketing Campaigns — EDA Insights

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on marketing campaign data to uncover actionable business insights related to campaign performance, audience behavior, budget efficiency, and customer engagement.

The analysis was designed for **marketing managers and business stakeholders** to support data-driven decision-making and optimize future marketing strategies.

The project includes:

* Data cleaning & preprocessing
* Outlier detection and treatment
* Feature engineering
* Statistical analysis
* Business insight generation
* Professional dashboard-style presentation

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The main goals of this analysis were to:

* Identify the highest-performing marketing channels
* Analyze the relationship between campaign spending and revenue
* Understand regional and audience-based performance differences
* Detect inefficient budget allocation and overspending
* Discover seasonal trends affecting campaign performance
* Improve customer targeting and retention strategies

## 🛠️ Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

Several data quality issues were identified and resolved:

* Standardized inconsistent categorical values
* Converted date columns from string to datetime format
* Corrected invalid date ranges
* Normalized multi-currency values into USD
* Detected and handled outliers using the IQR method
* Engineered additional KPIs and analytical features

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🔹 Channel Performance

* Certain channels significantly outperformed others in terms of ROI
* Budget allocation should prioritize high-performing channels

### 🔹 Spend vs Revenue

* Moderate positive correlation observed between spend percentage and revenue
* Higher spending does not always guarantee higher returns

### 🔹 Conversion Analysis

* Campaign type performance varied across geographic regions
* Regional behavior strongly impacts conversion efficiency

### 🔹 Seasonal Trends

* Peak months showed higher ROAS and conversions
* Campaign timing plays a critical role in marketing effectiveness

### 🔹 Customer Segmentation

* High Value and Loyal customers generated the largest revenue share
* Retention strategies provide stronger long-term ROI

### 🔹 Budget Control

* Some campaign managers consistently exceeded allocated budgets
* Budget monitoring and alert systems are recommended

### 🔹 Engagement & Bounce Rate

* Certain regions showed high bounce rates with low engagement
* Landing page optimization opportunities were identified
